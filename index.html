Month 1 — Build Your Foundation

Set up all systems before outreach begins. This phase determines the quality of everything that follows.

🗄️

CRM Setup & Contact Database

Deploy HubSpot Free or Zoho CRM. Import all existing customers. Create buyer persona lists segmented by product (CNG / Industrial / MEGC). Set up deal pipeline stages: Lead → Qualified → RFQ → Proposal → Order.

💼

LinkedIn Company Page Overhaul

Rewrite EKC company page with a keyword-rich description, custom banner showcasing all 3 products, and a Services section. Also optimize your personal LinkedIn profile for sales outreach — this is your primary prospecting tool.

📄

Product Collateral Creation

Design professional PDF datasheets for CNG, Industrial, and MEGC products using Canva Pro. Include technical specs, cylinder sizes, testing standards, certification logos, and a clear "Why EKC?" section. One page per product type.

🔍

Competitor & Market Research

Map top 10 global competitors — Luxfer Gas Cylinders, Worthington Industries, CP Industries, Everest Kanto (EKC India), Faber Industries. Identify pricing gaps, lead times, and weaknesses you can exploit in your pitch.

🏅

Certifications Audit & Showcase

Compile a complete list of all certifications held: ISO 11439, DOT, CE, TPED, BIS, etc. Add certification badge logos to ALL marketing material, website, and emails. Buyers evaluate compliance before price in industrial equipment.

Month 2 — Digital Presence & Directory Listings

Get discovered by buyers actively searching online. Build inbound lead flow before paid outreach begins.

🛒

B2B Marketplace Listings

Create verified supplier accounts on Alibaba (Gold Supplier), TradeIndia, ExportHub, Made-in-China, and IndiaMart. Upload full product catalogs with specs, certifications, MOQ, and shipping terms. Optimize titles for search keywords.

📚

Industry Directory Registrations

Register on Kompass.com, Thomasnet.com, NGVA Europe member directory, and CGA (Compressed Gas Association) resources. These directories are where professional procurement teams search — often ignored by competitors.

📧

Cold Email Infrastructure

Set up Brevo or Mailchimp free account. Create a 5-email cold outreach sequence with 3-day intervals. Write separate templates for CNG fleet operators, industrial gas buyers, and MEGC distributors. Keep every email under 250 words.

🌐

Website SEO Audit & Optimization

Target keywords: "CNG cylinder manufacturer UAE", "industrial cylinder exporter Dubai", "MEGC supplier Middle East". Optimize all product pages — title tags, meta descriptions, H1 headings, and image alt text. Check page load speed.

🎪

Trade Show Calendar & Early Registration

Confirm attendance at ADIPEC (Abu Dhabi, Nov), Gastech, CNG/LNG Americas, Africa Oil Week, and Hannover Messe. Book exhibition space now — early booking is significantly cheaper and secures better floor positions.

Month 3 — Launch Multi-Channel Outreach

Start active lead generation simultaneously across all channels. Volume and consistency are everything this month.

🎯

LinkedIn Sales Navigator Campaign

Subscribe to LinkedIn Sales Navigator ($99/mo). Build lists of Procurement Managers, Fleet Directors, and Plant Managers in target markets. Send 30–50 personalized connection requests per day. First message must offer value — never pitch immediately.

✉️

Cold Email Campaign — 500 Contacts

Use Apollo.io or Hunter.io to find verified contacts at CNG fleet operators in India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, and Latin America. Launch your 5-email sequence. Target: 25%+ open rate, 5%+ reply rate. Segment by country for personalization.

🤝

Agent & Distributor Identification

Identify 2–3 potential agents/distributors per priority country: India, Nigeria, Brazil, and Pakistan. Use UAE Chamber of Commerce, Dubai Exports network, and LinkedIn to find candidates. Prepare a standard Agent Agreement template.

📋

Government & Tender Portal Registrations

Register your company on UNGM (UN Global Marketplace), DG Market (EU), World Bank e-Procurement portal, and key national government procurement portals in target countries. Set up tender alerts for relevant product categories.

✍️

LinkedIn Authority Content

Publish 2 technical articles: "How to select the right CNG cylinder for fleet conversions" and "MEGC vs. tube trailers: a true cost comparison". These establish EKC as a technical authority and generate inbound interest passively.

Month 4 — Trade Shows & Partnership Deals

Maximize face-to-face engagement and formalize your agent network with signed agreements.

🏛️

ADIPEC / Key Trade Show Execution

Pre-book 20+ meetings via LinkedIn before the event. Man the booth with product brochures, cylinder samples, and certification documents. Collect 100+ business cards. Follow up by email within 24 hours — speed signals professionalism.

📝

Sign First Agent Agreements

Finalize and sign distribution agreements with 2–3 agents in India, Nigeria, and Brazil. Set clear quarterly volume targets, commission rates (typically 3–7% for industrial equipment), exclusivity terms, and reporting cadence.

🏛️

Dubai Exports Registration & Missions

Register with UAE Export Authority (dubaiexports.ae) for market missions, subsidized trade show participation, and direct buyer-seller matchmaking events. This is free government support paid for by your business license — most UAE companies never use it.

📞

Warm Lead Follow-up Campaign

Call and email all Month 3 leads. Qualify into Hot (RFQ in 30 days), Warm (3–6 months), Cold (6+ months). Send detailed quotation to Hot leads within 48 hours. Schedule Dubai factory visits for top-priority prospects.

💬

WhatsApp Business Broadcast Setup

Create a WhatsApp Business profile for EKC. Build broadcast lists for India, Middle East, and Africa contacts. Share product specs, price list updates, and RFQ links. Response rates in these markets are 3–5x higher than email.

Month 5 — Pipeline Nurturing & Proposals

Move hot leads through to quotation and order stage. This is where deals are won or permanently lost.

📊

Custom Proposal Template Creation

Build professional quotation templates for each product line. Include: pricing tiers, Incoterms (FOB/CIF Dubai), delivery timelines, quality certificate copies, payment terms (LC, TT, 30% advance), and warranty conditions.

✈️

Factory Visit Invitations

Invite your top 5–10 highest-value prospects for a Dubai facility tour. This converts hesitant buyers at a very high rate — seeing the operation, QC processes, and certifications firsthand removes objections that emails cannot address.

⭐

Customer Testimonials & Case Studies

Request written testimonials from 3–5 existing satisfied customers. Write a one-page case study for each product type showing: customer's challenge, EKC's solution, cylinders supplied, and measurable outcome. These are powerful in proposals.

🔄

Cold Lead Re-engagement Campaign

Email all leads marked Cold with a fresh angle: new certification achieved, product upgrade, competitive pricing update, or a relevant case study. A different context at a different moment can flip cold to warm.

📈

Mid-Point Performance Review

Analyze all channel data: LinkedIn response rates, email open/reply rates, trade show ROI, marketplace inquiries. Double down on the top 2 performing channels. Pause the weakest 2. Reallocate budget and effort before Month 6.

Month 6 — Scale & Close

Maximize conversions, close first orders, and set up the foundation for Year 2 exponential growth.

🏆

Close First Pilot Orders

Negotiate and close initial orders from your top 5 prospects — even small pilot orders of 50–100 cylinders. Offer slightly preferential pilot terms. The first order establishes a supplier relationship that grows year over year.

🔗

Referral Program Launch

Contact existing customers and offer a referral incentive: 2% volume discount on their next order for each new customer they introduce. Industrial supply networks are tight — a warm introduction converts at 5–10x the rate of cold outreach.

📑

Tender Bid Submissions

Submit formal bids for all open government and UN tenders identified in Month 3. Even losing bids build your submission track record, refine your documentation, and prequalify EKC for future tenders in the same portals.

🗺️

Year 2 Expansion Plan

Based on 6-month data, choose 3 new markets to enter in Year 2. Plan exhibition schedule, assign agent targets per market, set annual pipeline goal, and define the 2 new product verticals (e.g., hydrogen cylinders, fire suppression) to develop.

📱

LinkedIn Paid Retargeting Ads

Launch targeted LinkedIn ads to your website visitor audiences and profile viewers. Budget: $300–500/month. Target: Procurement and Operations decision-makers in your top 5 countries. In a 6–18 month sales cycle, staying visible is critical.
